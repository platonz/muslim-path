Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Muslim's Path ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free Islamic companion app providing prayer times, Quran reading, duas, and other Islamic tools. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — when you create an account: your email address and optionally your name. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and display name.
  • Location — only if you choose to set a location for prayer times. This is stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers.
  • Usage preferences — settings such as notification preferences, Quran reading position, and Dua favourites. These are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To sync your preferences across devices when you are signed in.
  • To send a one-time email confirmation when you register.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — for authentication and account storage. Your email is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Aladhan API (aladhan.com) — for prayer time calculations. Only your city name is sent; no personal data is transmitted.
  • Al-Quran Cloud API (alquran.cloud) — for Quran verse data. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Google OAuth (optional) — if you choose to sign in with Google. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

Most of your data (location, settings, favourites) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device unless you are signed in to an account. Account credentials are handled entirely by Supabase using industry-standard encryption.

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes. Muslim's Path is ad-free and has no tracking or analytics.

6. Children's Privacy

Muslim's Path is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests, contact: platoni@live.com
